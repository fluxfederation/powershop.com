Powershop
is changing

Powershop started out in 2009 selling power with a different proposition, Powershop, in a single market - New Zealand. Since then we've grown, with Powershop now retailing electricity in New Zealand, Australia and the UK.

But we don't want to stop there. We want to boldly go into new markets, which is why we've made the decision to separate Powershop retail from the software and technology part of the business.

The team that build the software that powers powershop is now flux.
