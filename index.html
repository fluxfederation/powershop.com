We're Powershop

Powershop is the world’s first online shop for electricity. Unlike traditional retailers, our customers can shop for power, get discounts and save money. They see how much they’re using and what it costs before they buy. It’s simple yet radical. Here’s what we’ve learnt about shifting the balance of power in the power industry.
