Why we’re a shit hot place to work

There is one thing all Powershop people have in common, and it’s the first thing we look for in future recruits — we all give a shit.

We want people who are passionate about what they do and who they do it for. Who believe that they can make a difference as an individual. People with the chutzpah to believe that they can radically transform the electricity industry by making customers love their power company. Powershop’s employees are a bunch of mavericks and free-thinkers who like to question the accepted norms of our industry in search of a better way. It makes for a dynamic workplace - it’s fast paced, fun, results-focused and rewarding.

Culture

Sure we've got the pool table, fancy coffee machine, beanbags and craft beer, and our CEO wears t-shirts (and pants, most days). But underneath the smart casual exterior we're a driven lot with some of Australasia’s brightest minds in technology, business and design. Our New Zealand HQ is in Wellington, our country’s equivalent of Silicon Valley, except that it’s way smaller and has cheaper rent.

Our award winning call centre is based in Masterton, a friendly country town just over the hill. Powershop Australia is based in Melbourne, where like Wellington it has access to a highly creative talent pool and really good coffee.

  • Head of Design - Wellington, NZ

    We’re looking for a new Head of Design to help us take Powershop to a more beautiful place.

    As Head of Design you will have responsibility for ensuring our brand and customer experience continues to pioneer New Zealand’s power industry, by engaging and growing our passionate fan-base by delivering our core proposition, giving Power To The People.

    This requires leading an in-house design team and external agencies in continuing to invent better ways to improve our customer experience by boosting engagement and helping them save. To achieve this you’ll be responsible for all work carried out by the design team, including technical development and delivery.

    As well as top-draw senior design experience, you’ll be capable of managing and motivating other visual and interaction designers, and directing Powershop’s user experience across all channels including its website, core application, and online and mobile communications.

    The role requires a ruthless focus on the end-user. The successful candidate will have experience in interaction design, visual design and skills critical in conceptualising and realising designs that match user expectations and standards of latest UI patterns and components.

    We’re looking for someone with plenty of experience, bags of talent and some serious attitude.
