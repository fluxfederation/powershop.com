Privacy Policy

Privacy Act 1993 ("the Act")

If we hold personal information about you, we comply with the Act. This Privacy Policy sets out information about how we do that. Further information about privacy and your rights under the Act can be found at www.privacy.org.nz.

In this privacy policy, when we refer to our website, we mean www.powershop.com. In this privacy policy, a reference to “we”, “us” or “our” means “Powershop New Zealand Limited” or “Powershop New Zealand Limited’s”, as appropriate.

Our online shop for this website is hosted by a company called Smallfish Limited (“Smallfish”). Smallfish has its own privacy policy available at: www.smallfish.co.nz/home/smallfish-privacy-policy. Our privacy policy is separate and in addition to Smallfish’s privacy policy. We aren’t responsible for Smallfish’s acts or omissions in relation to your data, so we recommend you also become familiar with Smallfish’s privacy policy. See also the section below headed ‘Information collected by Smallfish’.

What information we collect and how we collect it

While you use our website, including by requesting or purchasing products through our website, or when you communicate with us, we may collect and hold personal information about you. Powershop may collect information directly from our website or when we communicate with each other.

If you decide to try to sign up to purchase a product, and you enter your contact details but you do not successfully complete the ordering process, we may contact you to see if we can help you finish your order and/ or to get your feedback. If we do this, we will use the personal details you have entered on the website to contact you.

Powershop uses Google Analytic tools and cookies in the website to collect a range of information such as the number of visitors to our site per day, which pages they visit and when, some content they enter and see when they interact with our site, referring website address, their computer’s Internet Protocol address, operating systems, browser (which may contain device type) and language.

We use this information to analyse trends, better configure our service and messages, administer and track the performance of referring websites, our website and to understand your interactions with our website.

When Google Analytics receives personal information, that information is transferred, processed and stored on Google servers around the world in accordance with Google’s privacy policy available at www.google.com/privacy.html.

Information collected by Smallfish

Smallfish uses cookies and Google Services (including Google Analytics) to help Smallfish analyse how users use its website. Smallfish’s privacy policy refers to us as the “shop owner”, and you as our “customer”. Smallfish’s privacy policy mainly applies to us as shop owners but it also explains how it collects, holds, uses and may share information about you. When Google Analytics receives personal information, that information is transferred, processed and stored on Google servers around the world in accordance with Google’s privacy policy available at www.google.com/privacy.html.

From time to time, Smallfish may provide Powershop with (or Powershop may request) information in an aggregated form in which the customers cannot be identified. This helps us with analysis such as trends, sales patterns and your interaction with the shop.

Use of your information

The information we hold about you will be used to provide our products to you and for related purposes, for example:

  • confirming your identity;
  • processing any orders that you request through our website;
  • supplying you with products you have requested;
  • communicating with you;
  • complying with our obligations to you and other third parties involved in supplying products to you;
  • improving customer service;
  • providing you with information on third parties’ and/or Powershop’s other products and services;
  • conducting surveys and internal research;
  • complying with laws and regulations;
  • for our general business purposes (such as in aggregated data and to contact you if there are issues with your order);
  • for any other use for which you have given authorisation.

Powershop will not sell, lend, trade or otherwise disclose your personal information to any other person or agency except as set out in this privacy policy.

Disclosure of your information

Powershop will not share your personal information, except when:

  • you tell us we can do this;
  • we reasonably believe we are required to do so by law;
  • we need to do so to provide a product to you;
  • we want to keep you informed about other products;
  • we reasonably believe it is necessary to assist an adjudicator to resolve a complaint you make about us.

Other than as outlined above, we will not share your information with anyone else. You may ask us for access to any personal information we hold about you. If you think that information is inaccurate or incorrect, you may ask us to consider correcting or deleting it from our database. You can do this by sending an e-mail to the following address: shop@powershop.co.nz.

Storage and security of personal information We will comply with our obligations under the Act to use reasonable safeguards against loss, misuse or unauthorised disclosure. Most personal information will be held, stored and processed in New Zealand and/or Australia. Our staff has unique user identifications and passwords. We regularly train our staff on their responsibilities under the Act. Our physical premises are protected by reasonable security precautions. We do use some services which involve the transfer, processing and storage of some personal information outside of New Zealand- these are likewise protected by unique user identifications and passwords.

Email

Sometimes we send our customers information, such as a notification or confirmation about your requested products. We may do this by email. We may also send you by email newsletters or other information we think you may be interested in. Newsletters and other information you receive will have instructions for how you can remove yourself from that mailing list. If you ask to be removed from a mailing list, we will do this.

Cookies

A cookie is a small piece of information stored on your computer. Cookies help us improve your user-experience when you use our website. The technology we use mostly uses "session" cookies. Session cookies expire after 30 minutes of no activity on the Powershop website. However some cookies we use for internet statistics last longer. Cookies cannot damage files, nor can they read information from the hard drive of a computer. It is possible to disable the acceptance of cookies by your web browser. However, doing so may restrict your ability to access some web pages. Alternatively you can opt out of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt out page at www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp.

See the section on ‘What information we collect and how we collect it’ above to find out the sorts of things we use the cookies to track.

Links to other websites

This Privacy Policy only relates to this website and not our customer website (www.powershop.co.nz). Our website might have links to other websites. Powershop is not responsible for the practices on those other websites.

Changing this policy

We can change this privacy policy at any time. We will tell you about changes by posting an updated policy on our website, so we encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on privacy practices at Powershop for the purpose of this website. Changes apply 30 days from the date we post the change on our website. If you have any questions about our privacy policies or any other matter, please contact us.
