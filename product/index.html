Product

We’ve made electricity a consumer good

To make an intangible thing like electricity more visible to customers and help them know exactly how much they’re using, we sell it as a product in a shop. Customers can buy it in bundles of units, grab a special, or let Powershop buy it for them. They can get up-to-date information about their power usage and cost online, or from the emails we send. By breaking down consumption into a simple language of units, we’ve made it easy for people to control the amount of power they use, and given them a vocabulary to compare how they stack up against friends.

Our shop

Shopping for power helps people work out how much they need before they’re asked to pay for it. Rather than paying for power when the bill arrives, using an online shop gives customers flexibility and choice to:

  • Get specials when they’re available.
  • Pay for power when it suits them.
  • Pre-purchase power for future use to smooth out costs throughout the year.
  • Buy a combination of different Powerpacks – for example a special, some for the good of the environment and some for spring.

PowerpacksPower
Packs

Powershop sells electricity in blocks of power in varying sizes called Powerpacks. They give customers benefits no other power company offers. Customers can buy:

  • Green Packs for customers who want to do their bit for the environment.
  • Special Packs at low prices to help save money.
  • Top-Up Packs that can be used straight away.
  • Future Packs that let customers buy electricity for coming months, allowing them to stock up for winter and spread costs throughout the year.

Just a click a month

Every month we offer customers a cheap power deal, Simple Saver. Customers just need to click on an email link to receive an instant discount relative to their usage.

Keeping customers informed

Weekly email
Customers get a weekly email to let them know how much power they’re using and how it compares to the previous week. We also alert them about special deals.
Monthly email
Every month we send customers an email statement with their usage and cost for the month.
Login anytime
Customers can get up-to-date information about their power usage and cost anytime online.

Tracking usage and cost

Every customer has access to online tools so they see exactly how much power they’re buying and how many days it is likely to last. This makes planning and budgeting easier and takes the sting out of a monthly bill. Customers get:

  • Valuable insights into what times and activities are driving power usage.
  • More control over their power bill by making more informed decisions about how they use their power.
  • Awareness around their power use so they can reduce environmental impact.

Making payments easy

Powershop customers choose to pay before or after they use the power they buy. They can also choose if they want to pay by automatic payment via credit card or direct debit or make a manual payment using a number of payment options.
